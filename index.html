Developed By: Yuvaan Goel

Instructions For The Game

You need to hit the ball so that you don't lose a point. Move your racket down by moving your cursor down, and move your racket up by moving your cursor up. Everytime you hit the ball (or the computer hits it), the speed increases by 0.5. The first person to lose 5 points loses. Stay focused and have fun!
